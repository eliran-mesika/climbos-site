# ClimbOS Site Design Contract

- Follow the shared MesikaLabs public-site family language in `/Users/eliranmesika/Repos/mesikalabs-site/docs/design-language-system.md` for typography, legal-page chrome, spacing, accessibility, and compliance posture.
- Keep all ClimbOS HTML, CSS, assets, legal pages, and deployment configuration in this repository.
- Visual direction: practical gym companion. Use high-contrast, readable surfaces and real product proof when verified captures become available.
- Keep the first viewport concise and product-led. Avoid decorative marketing sections that obscure support, privacy, or terms access.
- Preserve local-first positioning. Do not imply account requirements, cloud sync, medical advice, injury diagnosis, or paid functionality unless current product and store truth support those claims.
- Use stable image dimensions, semantic HTML, one `h1`, sequential headings, visible focus states, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
- Keep the site static HTML/CSS/JS with no framework or analytics dependency.
- Use `climbos@mesikalabs.com` for app support, `privacy@mesikalabs.com` for privacy, `billing@mesikalabs.com` for billing, and `legal@mesikalabs.com` for legal requests.
